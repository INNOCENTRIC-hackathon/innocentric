The Pradhan Surakhsa Bima Yojana(PMSBY)

OVERVIEW

It is an accident insurance scheme, PMSBY offers a one-year accidental death and disability cover, which can be renewed annually. The cover is for a one-year period, starting June 1 to May 31 of subsequent year.

INSURANCE :-

Under PMSBY, the risk coverage available is Rs 2 lakh for accidental death and permanent total disability, and Rs 1 lakh for permanent partial disability. Permanent total disability is defined as total and irrecoverable loss of both eyes or loss of use of both hands or feet or loss of an eyesight and loss of use of a hand or a foot. Permanent partial disability is defined as total and irrecoverable loss of an eyesight or loss of use of a hand or foot.
Accidents, any death or disability (as defined under PMSBY) resulting from natural calamities is covered under PMSBY. While death due to suicide is not covered, that from murder is covered. Partial disability without irrecoverable loss of an eyesight or loss of use of one hand or foot is not covered. Also, the family gets no insurance benefit if the subscriber commits suicide.

ELIGIBILITY AND PAYMENTS :-

All individual (single or joint) bank account holders in the 18-70 year age group are eligible to join PMSBY.
Even NRIs are eligible, but if a claim arises, the claim benefit will be paid to the beneficiary/nominee only in Indian currency.
The premium payable is Rs 12 per annum per member and will be deducted from his bank account through an 'auto debit' facility in one instalment on or before June 1 every year.

WHERE TO GET THE SCHEME

The scheme is offered/administered through Public Sector General Insurance Companies (PSGICs) and other general insurance companies, in collaboration with participating banks. The banks are free to engage any general insurance company for implementing the scheme for their subscribers.
